### Description
TopRanker is the restaurant ranking app where your opinion actually matters. Unlike review platforms plagued by fake reviews and pay-to-play schemes, TopRanker uses a credibility-weighted voting system where experienced, trusted reviewers carry more influence.

**Key Features:**
- Trust-weighted rankings — not all votes are equal
- Credibility tiers that grow as you contribute honest reviews
- Head-to-head Challenger battles between restaurants
- City-based leaderboards for your local food scene
- Referral rewards for growing the trust network
- No fake reviews, no pay-to-play, no spam

**How It Works:**
1. Rate restaurants you've actually visited
2. Build your credibility through consistent, honest ratings
3. Watch your influence grow as you advance through tiers
4. Discover the best restaurants ranked by people who know food

Join the trust revolution. Your taste matters.
