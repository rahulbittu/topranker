# TopRanker — Category Expansion Roadmap

**Status:** PLANNING — Not immediate priority
**Owner:** Rahul Pitta (CEO)
**Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## Summary

This document catalogues 20 candidate categories for TopRanker's expansion
beyond restaurants. All categories follow the "Best [Specific Thing] in [City]"
pattern.

**Current status of all non-restaurant categories: DISCUSSION PHASE**

No category moves to SPRINT READY without all Category Readiness Checklist
answers documented. No category moves to BUILDING unless restaurants are healthy.

## Priority Rounds

**Round 1 (low risk, high architectural overlap):**
- Cafés & Coffee, Barbershops, Bakeries & Desserts

**Round 2 (moderate complexity, strong DFW market):**
- Hair Salons, Nail Salons, Tutors & Education, Photographers, Event Venues

**Round 3 (strong trust case, higher complexity):**
- Auto Care, Home Services, Spas, Pet Care, Gyms

**Hold for legal review:**
- Doctors & Clinics, Schools & Daycares

Full spec with 20 categories, readiness checklists, and discussion process
is maintained in this file. See the complete document for details on each
category's sub-item patterns, rating dimensions, risk levels, and team
discussion questions.

## Guiding Principle

The restaurant leaderboard is the product. Everything else is the platform
vision. Prove the restaurant loop first, then expand one category at a time.
