# PR & Launch Strategy

**Created:** Sprint 209 (2026-03-09)
**Owner:** Jasmine Taylor (Marketing)

## Core Narrative

**Tagline:** "Rankings you can trust."

**Elevator Pitch:** TopRanker is a restaurant ranking app that uses credibility-weighted voting to eliminate fake reviews and pay-to-play schemes. Unlike Yelp or Google Reviews, not all votes are equal — experienced, trusted reviewers carry more influence, creating rankings that actually reflect quality.

**Problem Statement:** Restaurant review platforms are broken. Fake reviews, paid placements, and algorithm manipulation mean the "best" restaurant isn't actually the best. Consumers know it. Restaurants know it. But nobody's fixed it — until now.

**Solution:** TopRanker's trust-weighted system gives more influence to reviewers who demonstrate consistency, local knowledge, and honest behavior. Your credibility grows as you contribute, and the rankings improve with every honest review.

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Food-conscious urban millennials (25-40)
- Frustrated with Yelp/Google review manipulation
- Use restaurant apps weekly
- Value authenticity and transparency
- Active on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter

### Secondary: Food bloggers and local food influencers
- Want their expertise to matter more
- Currently feel drowned out by fake reviews
- Can drive significant referral traffic
- Active across social platforms

### Tertiary: Restaurant owners
- Tired of pay-to-play review platforms
- Want fair representation of quality
- Willing to pay for honest exposure
- Business Pro and Challenger features appeal
